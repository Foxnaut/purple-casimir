In Broccoli Distro, your goal is to feed the in-need communities around the world.

To gain food click on the broccoli image. With food, you can feed the communities around the world and buy power-ups that are related to real-world solutions to food insecurities.

Watch out for in-game events including droughts, economic downturns, famine, and crop failure which are some of the leading causes of food insecurities.

Keep the citizens of the world alive as long as possible to be awarded the most amount of points.

Make sure to click the info buttons to learn more about food insecurities and in-game mechanics.
